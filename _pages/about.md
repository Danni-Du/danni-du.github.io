I am Danni Du (杜旦尼), a postdoctoral research associate in the Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences Program at Princeton University. My Ph.D. advisors are Dr. Aneesh Subramanian and Dr. Weiqing Han. The main focus of my research work is to improve the prediction skill and understand the predictability in the tropics at subseasonal time scales. I am particularly interested in the Madden-Julian Oscillation (MJO) and the Indian Summer Monsoon (ISM), and the role that air-sea interaction plays in these phenomena.
